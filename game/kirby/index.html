Kirby's Dream Land (1992)

Description

Dedede, King of Dream Land, is a penguin-like creature who is too gluttionous for his own good. Gathering food for his midnight feast, he got carried away and ended up taking
all the food from the inhabitants of his country. One of them, a pink little guy named Kirby, decides to travel all the way to King Dedede's palace and retrives the lost food by any means.

Kirby's Dream Land is a side-scrolling platform action game, and the first in the Kirby series. Although the protaganist appears to be a weak little marshmallow, he is actually quite powerful:
he has the ability to suck up his enemies like a vacuum and then shoot them out at other enemies. Kirby can also swallow and inflate himself, which allows him to fly.

There are four levels in the game; no save feature is available.
